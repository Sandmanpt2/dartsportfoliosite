Welcome to the Victoria Withington Darts team !

Here at the Vic we love to socialise, relax, and most of all chuck some arrows !

Come learn about the Vic's rich history !

The Vic was founded in 1898, named after Queen Victoria

It has been a haven to meet friends, new or old, and to get away from the many troubles of society.

Throughout the wars it was used to house orphans and other vulnerable children

The Victoria has had a place in darts since it's birth

This was the very first team in South Manchester, formed in 1902 ! They are commonly known as the Gazz's.

Whether you're an expert or have never chucked before, we welcome all ages and skill levels !
